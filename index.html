About Me



Hi, I'm Jake. I live in Portland, Oregon with my wife, dog, and cat. I grew up on the south shore of Massachusetts and have been slowly migrating west ever since. First college in upstate New York, then a few years skiing in Idaho and Wyoming until making my way to Oregon for law school. I am driven by curiosity and my love of learning new things. I find it immensely satisfying to complete a difficult project that required me to expand my knowledge and abilities. Lately I have been interested in the nexus between software and the traditional role of lawyers. To that end I have committed myself to learn how to code and I am excited to see where this path takes me.
